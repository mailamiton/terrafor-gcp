Manuraj Jain, Founder & CEO

Manuraj is an engineer from IIT Kgp and an MBA from INSEAD, France. In his early days, he worked for Schlumberger as an oilfield engineer. Later he joined a startup, Intellecap, and worked on strategy, research, and capital restructuring for MFIs in India. Post his MBA, he founded Vinculum Capital Partners, a strategy and capital boutique firm, and The Money Club, India’s first AI-powered P2P Group Savings Platform in 2016.

Surajit Ray, Co-Founder & CTO

Surajit is a biotech engineer from IIT Kgp and joined Schlumberger right out of college. He has over 19 years of experience with expertise in Solution Architecture, Predictive Data Analytics, and Cloud Deployments at scale. He was a technical architect for Apollo Munich Health Insurance, an industry leader in the Health insurance industry. Apart from this, he has been CTO for 4 other startups with the latest one being The Money Club, India’s first AI-powered P2P Group Savings Platform.

Debashish Das, Advisor

Debashish is a Business and HR expert having worked in the Corporate and SME space extensively. He worked with leading brands as Head of HR, after which he has been active in SME HR space since the last 11 years as an entrepreneur. He brings with him total experience of 26 years. He is an alumni of IIM C,BITS Pilani and IMS Ghaziabad. He is a certified independent director, executive and business coach, advisor for Loyal VC. As an advisor of Moneyclub Technologies Private Limited, he has been instrumental to advise and conceptualize Vrddi Employee Financial Wellbeing (EFW) category as part of the founding team from day one.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Vrddi?
    “Vrddi is a tech enabled employee cohort financial wellness product which solves the problem of cash in need of an employee by not taking loans to meet additional expenses”
  • How is it unique?
    It is the only tech based transparent solution available anywhere to offer cash pay out of nearly 10 times(conditional) the pay in. Further it covers over 60 categories of disbursal. Such large category of interest free cash disbursal is not available elsewhere.
  • What is the basic requirement to implement Vrddi?
    User employee needs a smart phone to use Vrddi app. Nothing Else.
  • How safe is it?
    It is 100% safe and compliant to operate by organizations and commercial establishments.
  • Is there any documentation needed?
    Basic ID KYC documents which will be managed through the company HR.
  • What is the activation process?
    The company HR or senior representative may write a mail to contactus@vrddi.com requesting a demo and the rest will be taken care of.
  • How will the payout happen?
    The payout will happen based upon the technology based algorithm and the money will be credited to the beneficiary account on salary day through the payroll process.
  • What is the maximum time I can get the payout benefit?
    The maximum possibility is 4 times *(based upon plan) a year for an employee to get payout benefit.
  • Can I drop out of the plan midway?
    Longer duration of the cohort is gainful for all, in case of drop out the deposit amount will be accordingly adjusted amongst all other cohort members.
  • What size of organization may adopt Vrddi?
    Any Size SME to Corporate may adopt Vrddi to solve the employee financial wellness issue.
